What We Do

Empowering citizens to shape the laws that govern them

Draft Legislation Access

Browse and review draft bills and policies before they become law. Get early access to proposed legislation at national, state, and local levels.

Public Commentary

Share your perspective directly with policymakers. Your feedback is collected, analyzed, and presented to legislative bodies for consideration.

Verified Participation

Secure authentication ensures one person, one voice. Your identity is protected while your opinion is validated and counted.

Transparent Process

Track how public sentiment influences policy decisions. See real-time analytics on community feedback and legislative responses.

How It Works

Simple steps to make your voice heard

1

Register & Verify

Sign up as a citizen using Aadhaar authentication or as an official with government credentials. Your identity is verified but your privacy is protected.

2

Browse Legislation

Explore draft bills filtered by your interests, location, or impact area. Read simplified summaries or dive into full legislative text.

3

Share Your Views

Comment on specific provisions, suggest amendments, or express support or concerns. Your feedback is categorized and analyzed.

4

Track Impact

See how community feedback influences final legislation. Get notified when bills you've commented on are updated or passed.
